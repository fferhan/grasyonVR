Frequently Asked Questions

We are curious about your opinions about Grasyon VR and we would be very happy if you give us feedback about Grasyon VR.

  • Will Grasyon VR do everything about virtual reality glasses, including hardware, software, design?

    At first it is a little difficult for us to do everything. That's why we split the Grasyon into parts and each department will work in its own area (having basic knowledge from other areas as well). Thus, when we combine all fields, GRASYON will emerge.

  • How can I enter the Grasyon universe?

    You will be able to enter the Grasyon universe both from a laptop and with virtual reality glasses. You will be able to access the opportunities offered by the Grasyon universe on both platforms. However, entering the Grasyon universe with virtual reality glasses will improve your experience.

  • In which sector will Grasyon VR serve?

    Grasyon VR will first change the fate of the trade industry and create 100% virtual shopping platforms. Users will be able to shop and those who want to become entrepreneurs will be able to earn money by establishing their own virtual offices/shops.

  • How will consumers be able to pay for the goods and services they purchase?

    Customers will be able to pay with both crypto money and banknotes of their choice. Not every cryptocurrency will be valid for the security of the system.

  • When virtual characters buy an apparel, how will they know if the item is on their body?

    Users will be able to test on their virtual characters whether the clothes they will buy will fit on their bodies by taking 3 photos from the system and uploading them to the system.
